I have also worked on a Vertically Integrated Project (VIP) at Georgia Tech.
The goal of this team was to see trends in the data of MOOC (massively open online courses) held at Georgia Tech and help students not give up on their courses.
We ran multiple models trying to guess what final grade they would end up and if they would even finish the course.
We then started to upload these models and the information we had in the form of infographics onto a web application that professors and students alike could access to enhance their online learning experience.
